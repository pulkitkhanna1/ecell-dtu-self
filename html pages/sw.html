Startup Weekend'19

plan create execute

day 1


+
elevator pitch

The air crackled with electricity as budding entrepreneurs shared their platform with like minded people with one common goal - to launch a start-up in 54 hours. Startup Weekend'19 resuscitated with the participants full of vigor and their exciting new ideas, ready to lay a foundation to their dreams – to build their own company and that too within 54 hours!

+
networking dinner

There was a Networking dinner where the participants got a chance to meet with their mentors, their rivals and their teammates. A chance to gel up and open up with people you need to work with the next 54 hours is what networking dinner was all about. A platform where like minded shared their thoughts, ideas and opinions.

+
team formation

The stage was set and teams were formed. Everybody was prepared for the No talk All Action plan. Teams resonating myriad vibes of competition, innovation and out of the box ideas ready to be transformed into exceptional startups.

day 2


+
mentor connect

Mentors including Deepshikha Anand, Sameer Mathur, Arindam Nath, Nikhil Gupta, Charru Khanna and Varija Bajaj carefully analyzed the idea and provided the teams with a 'Success Mantra' and insights on how to deal with the business competition and reach on top of their game.

+
hustle

The teams started to hustle hard to transform dreams into reality, brainstorming ideas, the startup weekend resonated with different energies of the teams with only one goal that is to form a startup in 54 hours.

+
execution

All that glitters in not gold but all that glitters often catch the eye and remain in sub conscience for a long time. The next step was to present the ideas in an innovative manner so as to catch the attention of the audience, mentors and the judges. The plan was in process to transform into action.

day 3


+
mentor connect

The final pitch was presented before the esteemed panel of judges who were Mr.Amit Gupta (VP mastercard), Mr. Gaurav Malhotra (founder ann patra), Mr.Sunil Solanki (Country Head SAP), Mr.Neeraj Singh (Director KPMG) and Mr. Rajeev Sikka (CEO Adani Gas).

+
Presentation

All the hustle and hard work finally bore fruit. The nerve wracking competition had begun where the teams had to compete with the best to win the final lap. The atmosphere was full of tension, vigor and zest to deliver their best pitch possible. With every participant longing for a taste of victory the results were finally announced.

+
celebration

Even though there was only one winning team but every individual had a sense of pride in their mind for they have experienced something which is rare- the power of teamwork, creative thinking, pressure handling and most importantly the essence of creating their own company from scratch within 54 hours.
