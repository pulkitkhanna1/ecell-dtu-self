UDAAN E-SUMMIT 2020

About E-Summit

E’Summit by E-Cell DTU is the largest entrepreneurship promoting event of Northern India. The event has served host to some of the most well-known personalities of the industry over the years. It continues to cultivate and nurture numerous ideas every year, and provide an extraordinary platform putting forward the youth to exchange their ideas with their corporate idols, giving them insights and lessons about the world of business.

Why Attend

Eloquent Orators

E-Cell hosts some of the most articulate and accomplished personnel of our country to make sure that the attendees are able to leave with a plethora of knowledge as well as some experience to deal with real-life problems.

Competitive Challenges

Who doesn't like to end a competitive challenge by lifting the winning trophy? An astounding line-up of competitions including Business Plan Competition and overnight Business Hackathon waiting for you at E-Summit'20 with numerous rewards to be won. These one of a kind bewildering competitions present our budding entrepreneurs with enormous exposure and learning.

Speedy Internships

Our Startup Internship and Job Fair provides opportunities for quick internship interviews as well as job placements to all attendees. With more than 700 internship profiles the fair acts as the first step for numerous careers every year.

Summit Schedule

DAY 1

Leadership & Disruption

Day 1 begins with the leadership lecture series wherein speakers, entrepreneurs, leaders, and all those who made an impact with glorious achievements in their spectrum of work share their career journey to inspire the attendees. Top industrialists of our country impart various tips and techniques that the youth may utilize to make it big in their careers.

YouTube Entrepreneur

After the inspirational session of Leadership Lecture Series, we have a session on more modern forms of entrepreneurship namely youtube entrepreneurship, various YouTubers and social media influencers share their experiences with the massive crowd assembled at the Open Amphitheatre. We have had the pleasure of hosting various content creators such as Harsh Beniwal and Slay point for this event.

Comedy Night

To conclude day 1 of E-Summit on a lighter note, a Comedy Evening is held right after the Youtube entrepreneurship session. The evening begins with a short performance of a local comedian followed by the main act and concluded with an improv session with some of the top improv artists of the industry. We have had the pleasure of having numerous top comedians of India, such as Biswa Kalyan Rath and Kenny Sebastien perform and exhilarate the audience with their wit and humor.

DAY 2

UDAAN Startupthon

Day 2 marks the beginning of the most ideal showcase event that provides an avenue to thousands of startup enthusiasts of our to exhibit their business acumen, creativity, and innovation - The UDAAN Startupthon. One of the flagship events of E-Cell DTU, it aims to promote the spirit of Entrepreneurship. Small scale startups looking for incubation and funding are given a chance to present their pitch to a panel of esteemed judges and receive help in incubation, funding as well as branding.

watch video
DAY 3

CrackIt: HR Conundrum

Day 3 begins with a revolutionary session of student HR interaction. Students are given expert advice from the HRs of Big Companies, mock interviews are conducted, information about Resume’ building and group discussions are held to prepare the students for real-world corporate scenarios.

Startup Internship & Job Fair

In the afternoon Startup Internship Fair an annual internship caricature is organized by E-Cell in close collaboration with various startups in the country. Startups get a chance to interact and hire students through speed interviews. It is a great opportunity for startups to hire students from almost all backgrounds (Technical, Non-Technical, Management, etc).

watch video

Band Night

The day is finally concluded with a band night to end the event on a fun note. Members of the E-Cell dance with their friends and cool off their fatigue after working tirelessly to deliver the most highly acclaimed entrepreneurship summit of Northern India.
